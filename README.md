# Bcured - Health Connect App

![Green Leaves Icon Health Logo](https://github.com/Rohanraj123/Bcured/assets/96484809/4b64164c-ebfe-4754-bf9f-66193dae8dbb)

Bcured is a comprehensive health connect app designed to simplify your healthcare experience. With powerful Machine Learning (ML) algorithms, secure data storage, and personalized health recommendations, Bcured empowers you to take control of your well-being.


## Features

- **Medical Report Analysis**: Effortlessly analyze your medical reports using advanced ML algorithms.
- **Personalized Health Recommendations**: Receive actionable insights based on your medical data.
- **Secure Medical Records**: Safeguard your medical history in a secure digital repository.
- **Robust Authentication**: Ensure your data is accessible only to authorized individuals.
- **Connect with Healthcare Professionals**: Share reports for remote consultations.
- **Track Your Health Journey**: Monitor progress and visualize improvements over time.
- **Accessible Anywhere**: Manage your health on the go.

## Getting Started

Follow these steps to get started with Bcured:

1. **Clone the Repository**: `git clone https://github.com/YourUsername/Bcured.git`

2. **Install Dependencies**: `cd Bcured && npm install`

3. **Configure Authentication**: Set up authentication for secure access.

4. **Run the App**: `npm start`
